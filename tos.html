02 App Description

Statifly is a free Android utility application that provides the following features:

  • Status Saver — View and save WhatsApp & WhatsApp Business status updates (images and videos) to your device.
  • Vault — Organise saved statuses into folders with search functionality.
  • Creator — Create custom status images with text, stickers, backgrounds, and camera capture.
  • Elevate — Create product showcase reels from your gallery images with customisable templates.
  • Share Import — Receive images, videos, and links shared from other apps, with link preview resolution and save-to-vault functionality.
  • Backup & Restore — Create encrypted backup archives of your statuses and restore them on the same device.
  • Storage Manager — Monitor and manage the app's disk usage across cache, saved, and created statuses.
